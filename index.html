Our Commitment to the Ocean

Peware Care was created with a simple belief: businesses should give back to the environment that supports them.

Vietnam’s oceans and rivers are among the most valuable ecosystems in the world, yet they face increasing threats from plastic pollution and waste.

To contribute to meaningful change, Peware commits 1% of its profits to environmental initiatives focused on protecting marine and river ecosystems.

Through environmental partnerships, recycling programs, and community action, every Peware purchase supports real change.

Working Together for a Cleaner Future

Protecting the environment requires collaboration. Peware Care works with environmental organizations, recycling companies, volunteer groups, and community networks. Together, we are building a stronger ecosystem for environmental protection.

Transparency Matters

Peware Care believes environmental impact must be measurable and transparent. We track and document:

  • Waste collected & sorted
  • Project locations mapped
  • Partner organizations
  • Community participation rates
  • Environmental impact progress

Regular impact updates ensure that every contribution leads to real and visible change.
